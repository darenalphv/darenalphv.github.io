About Daren

Award-winning Full Stack Tech Generalist, CEO, and Top Technology & AI Expert in Malaysia.

Daren Tan is the founder and CEO of ALPHV Group of Companies — a Kuala Lumpur-based technology firm delivering enterprise-level digital transformation and practical AI implementation across financial, health, hospitality, property, insurance, legal, and medicolegal industries.

With 10 years in the tech industry and a Masters in Data Science by Research, Daren is ranked #7 on Favikon Malaysia's Top 50 Tech Creators on LinkedIn (2026) and has been featured 5× in The Star, 3× on BFM 89.9, Malaymail, and Digital News Asia.

He founded two prominent Malaysian tech communities — Developer Kaki and Hackathon Kaki — and is a 10× hackathon podium finisher, 20+ invited judge, and 30+ invited speaker across Malaysia and Southeast Asia.
